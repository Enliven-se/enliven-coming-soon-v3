You are the Talent!

We share the revenue. Did we mention, you get 70%!

Like all other industries publishing is a talent business. Why is it then that so many gifted people are paid so little, and almost all the money is spent on expensive publishing executives, fancy headquarters and shareholder profits?

You are the talent! By building a new generation publishing model, ENLIVEN contributors will share 70% of our revenues (mainly through subscriptions and native and display ads).

ENLIVEN has the long-term vision to be the best paying publishing platform in the world. Sure it will (just like Spotify and other pioneers) take some time, but if we work as a team we will get there.

Retain Your Copyrights

It's your creative work - why give it away?

Regardless if it's an old "evergreen" or a freshly developed article that you are publishing for the first time, you will retain all copyright to your material and are totally free to move it from ENLIVEN if you’re not happy with us or find greener pastures.

We believe that you should be able to make money on your creative work from multiple platforms, thereby creating many separate recurring revenue streams.

Very much like Instagram, blog portals, Vimeo, and other popular channels, you are the talent and ENLIVEN is just one of these enablers. Find the platforms that are best at bringing your creative work to the right crowd that cares, and roll with it.
