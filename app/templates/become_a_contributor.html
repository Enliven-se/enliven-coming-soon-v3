You are the Talent!

We share the revenue. Did we mention, you get 70%!

Publishing is a talent business (like any other industry). So why is it so that so many gifted people are paid so little and that much of the money ends up with expensive publishing executives, fancy headquarters and shareholder profits?

You are the talent! By building a new generation publishing model, ENLIVEN contributors will share 70% of our total revenues (mainly subscriptions and ad’s).

ENLIVEN has the long-term vision to be the best paying publishing platform in the world. Sure it will (just like Spotify and the likes) take some time, but if we work as a team we will get there.

Retain Your Copyrights

It's your creative work - why give it away?

Regardless of an old evergreen or a newly developed article that you publish for the first time, you will of course retain all copyrights to your material and are totally free to move it from ENLIVEN if you're not happy with us.

We believe that you should be able to make money on your creative work on multiple platforms and thereby creating many separate recurring revenue streams

Very much like Vimeo, Instagram, blog portals, etc. you're the talent and ENIVEN is just one of these enablers. Find the platforms that are best at bringing out your creative work to your crowd and roll with it.
